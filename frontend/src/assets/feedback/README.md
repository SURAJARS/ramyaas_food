## Image Requirements

- **Format**: JPEG or PNG
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (mobile phone screenshot aspect ratio)
- **Dimensions**: Recommended 540x960px or similar
- **Content**: Customer testimonials, reviews, or product photos from satisfied customers
- **Quality**: Clear, well-lit phone screenshots

## Adding Customer Feedback

1. Take phone screenshots of customer feedback/testimonials
2. Rename them according to the structure above
3. Place them in this folder
4. The FeedbackSection component will automatically display them with:
   - Auto-cycling between images on hover (1.5s intervals)
   - Fade transitions
   - Image indicators
   - Rotation and hover effects

The component handles up to 5 images per customer card.
